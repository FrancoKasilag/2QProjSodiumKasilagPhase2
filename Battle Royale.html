What is Battle Royale Tetris?

What is a Battle Royale?

Some context before this: A "battle royale" game, such as PUBG, Fortnite, and
Apex Legends, traditionally puts a certain amount of players in a map, often with
guns or other weapons, and has them fight to the death to see which player or
team is left standing (yes, like squid game). This means that the players with the
most skill in the context of what the game demands will often survive longer and
probably win.

What does Battle Royale tetris look like?

Now take that image, and replace the guns with puzzle pieces and the map with a
giant lobby of people playing Tetris, and you get the most popular Tetris Battle Royale,
Tetris 99 (this isn't the only free-for-all Tetris game, but it's easily the most iconic).
For the most part it uses the same garbage as PvP Tetris, but your garbage gets sent to
random people and someone's garbage randomly gets sent to you. If you top out, you lose,
so survive as long as possible as the Tetriminos fall faster and faster.
